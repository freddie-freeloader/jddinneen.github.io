Service

I am proud to support information science (info studies, LIS, etc) and the GLAMR sector (galleries, libraries, archives, museums) through invited and volunteer work and public outreach.

My full service history is available upon request of my CV, and most of my peer review can be seen on Publons, but some highlights include:

  • For the last few years I have been a section editor for Open Information Science and an officer on the ASIS&T special interest group for the History and Foundations of Information Science (SIG HFIS).
  • I have been an invited juror for ASIS&T awards (twice) and an invited guest editor for Education for Information.
  • I regularly provide peer review for various international journals (e.g. JASIST) and conferences (e.g. ACM CHI).
  • I periodically get invited, and am delighted to provide, public and industry outreach in the form of presentations and interviews. In 2020 I had the pleasure to speak at the New Zealand National Library.
