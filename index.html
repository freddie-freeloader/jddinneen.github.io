Prof Jesse David Dinneen, PhD

I am a newly appointed Junior Professor in the School of Library and Information Science at Humboldt University in Berlin. Previously, I was a Senior Lecturer at Victoria University in Wellington, New Zealand (2017-2020). I did my PhD (and MLIS) at McGill University, where I was a Richard H. Tomlinson Doctoral Fellow. I also have some formal training in philosophy from UMass Boston (BA) and King’s College London (MA).

While my Humboldt office and email are being set up, I can be contacted at jesse.dinneen@mail.mcgill.ca.
